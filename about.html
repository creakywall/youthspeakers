about us

YouthSpeakers.org is a non-profit organization created for two purposes. First, to provide youth group leaders across the country a resource to find quality speakers for their youth groups. Second, to provide speakers an opportunity to use their talents and skills to serve their communities. To help with hosting and design costs, sponsers display their advertisements throughout the website.

This project was inspired by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, Brigham Young University’s international tour group Young Ambassadors, and the Self Expression and Leadership Program at Landmark Education.

If you are interested in being a part of this on-going project as a speaker, staff, or street team, please contact us. We welcome any assistance. If you are interested in donating funds to this cause, we greatly appreciate your donations. Any funds received will be used for equipment for presentations, speakers’ travel expenses, and upkeep of the web-site.
